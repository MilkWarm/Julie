Julie is a shoegaze band originating from Orange County, California. Their first and only album is My Anti-Aircraft Friend, although they have two other EP's and
a single, flutter. Starting in 2018, they've released new music pretty consistently, and each song is enjoyable in my opinion.


This is their website right here , looks a lot like something I'd make right now!

The members of julie include:

  • Keyan Pourzand: Vocals and Guitar
  • Alex Brady: Vocals and Bass
  • Dillon Lee: Drums
